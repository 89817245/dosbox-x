00020     1. Registers are compatible with YM3812 (OPL2) FM sound source.

00022     3. 18 simultaneous melody sounds, or 15 melody sounds with 5 rhythm sounds (with two operators).
00023     4. 6 four-operator melody sounds and 6 two-operator melody sounds, or 6 four-operator melody

00036 - sinus table is a little different: the negative part is off by one...
00037

00039   one must set bit 5 in register 0x01(test).
00040   on OPL3 this bit is ignored and 4-waveform select works *always*.
00041   (Don't confuse this with OPL3's 8-waveform select.)

00046

00048   output of operator 2 on OPL3; on OPL and OPL2 output of operator 1

01015           - depends on the channel 6 'connect' register:
01016               when connect = 0 it works the same as in normal (non-rhythm) mode (op1->op2->out)
01017               when connect = 1 _only_ operator 2 is present on output (op2->out), operator 1 is ignored
01018           - output sample always is multiplied by 2

01022

01024         SLOT = &CH[6].SLOT[SLOT1];
01025         env = volume_calc(SLOT);
01026

01028         SLOT->op1_out[0] = SLOT->op1_out[1];

01050         // HH  (13) channel 7->slot 1 combined with channel 8->slot 2 (same combination as TOP CYMBAL but different output phases)

01052         // TOM (14) channel 8->slot 1
01053         // TOP (17) channel 7->slot 1 combined with channel 8->slot 2 (same combination as HIGH HAT but different output phases)
01054

01056         // HH  channel 7->slot1
